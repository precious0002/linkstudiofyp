LINK STUDIO

About

Link Studio is a simple web app that lets people sketch, brainstorm, and share ideas together on a digital canvas. Link Studio makes collaboration easy and accessible. With just a link, anyone can join a canvas, draw in real time, and bring their ideas to life alongside others
